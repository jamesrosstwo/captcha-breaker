Question: Which cannons are actively firing or have muzzle smoke?
Positive Prompt: Add billowing smoke, fiery blast streams and hot gases erupting from the cannon's open muzzle and barrel to indicate it has just discharged its payload.
Negative Prompt: Extinguish and dissipate any smoke, flames or discharge from the cannon, leaving the muzzle clear, empty and undisturbed without signs of recent firing.
